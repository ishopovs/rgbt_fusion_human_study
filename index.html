Pedestrian localisation task

Read the instructions, then press Start.
Instructions
The goal of the trial is to evaluate the speed with which a person can detect a VRU (pedestrian, cyclist...) in the output of different fusion methods.
Some images will be pure RGB images, no fusion.
Please take time to complete this session without distractions, because your response time is measured and used for evaluation.
How it works:
- You will see a sequence of images in different driving conditions.
- Click on each pedestrian you can see (multiple clicks allowed).
- When done with the image, press Space (or click Next) to continue.
- Try to respond as quickly and accurately as possible.
