Instructions

The goal of this task is to evaluate how quickly a person can detect a vulnerable road user (VRU) in the output of different imaging methods (RGB and thermal fusion outputs).

  • Complete the session without distractions (reaction time is measured).
  • You will see a sequence of driving images recorded in low light conditions.
  • Click each pedestrian/cyclist you can see (multiple clicks allowed).
  • When done, press Space to submit and continue.
  • Try to respond as quickly and accurately as possible.

Tip: keep the browser window maximised. Use a mouse/trackpad (touch devices are not recommended).
